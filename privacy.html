Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Overview

Beyond Vision is designed with privacy at its core. The app processes everything on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

Beyond Vision does not collect or transmit:

  • Camera footage or photos
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Personal information of any kind

On-Device Processing

All AI processing — object detection, image classification, saliency analysis, and OCR — is performed entirely on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's Vision and CoreML frameworks. No camera frames, images, or results are sent to any external server.

Camera Access

Beyond Vision requests access to your device's camera for the Live Mode feature. Camera data is processed locally in real time and is never stored or transmitted. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library only when you choose to import an image in Explore Mode. Selected photos are processed locally and are never uploaded or stored outside your device.

Haptic & Settings Data

Your Haptic Tuner preferences (intensity, sharpness, toggles) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Beyond Vision does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Third-Party Services

Beyond Vision uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services that collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
